How to use The Food Recall App

  1. Begin by entering the desired search item name


  2. Next select the search type between using the item's barcode, product name, or company name.


  3. Continue by choosing a date range that the item might have been recalled, by selecting the calendar drop down tab.


  4. Select the "Search" button and allow results to load.


  5. Proceed by looking at the "Results Section" where the desired item search will display listing: Date, Product, Recalling Firm, and Reason for recall.


  6. Scrolling down further on the page will also display the "Latest Searched Recalls" section. That will display the most recently recalled items searched by other app users.


  7. After getting your desired results restart the process to search for another recall!
